Mission, Vision, and Core Values

Learn what guides SCSLAI in serving members, growing responsibly, and staying grounded in trust.

MISSION

Serve members with care

TO DELIVER QUANTITY, INNOVATIVE FINANCIAL PRODUCTS AND SERVICES TO THE MEMBERS, AND PURSUE BENCHMARKING IN OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY, RISK MANAGEMENT AND PROFITABILITY.

VISION

Grow responsibly

THE MOST PREMIER NON-STOCK SAVINGS AND LOAN ASSOCIATION IN THE PHILIPPINES THAT VALUES INTEGRITY, CORPORATE GOVERNANCE, PRUDENCE OF SAVINGS AND DELIVERY OF OPTIMUM RETURNS AND INVESTMENTS THROUGH THE CADRE OF EMPOWERED AND COMPETENT EMPLOYEES, OFFICERS AND BOARD OF TRUSTEES.

CORE VALUES

Lead with integrity

We value INTEGRITY, PROFESSIONALISM, DEPENDABILITY, CLIENT-DRIVEN, and SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY to the welfare of our members and organization.
