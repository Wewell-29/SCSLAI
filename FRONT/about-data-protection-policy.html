Data Privacy Statement

Committed to protecting your personal and financial information.

Learn how the Supreme Court Savings and Loan Association, Inc. (SCSLAI) collects, uses, stores, shares, and safeguards your personal information in accordance with Republic Act No. 10173, otherwise known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

Our Commitment

At Supreme Court Savings and Loan Association, Inc. (SCSLAI), we understand the importance of protecting your personal and financial information. Maintaining your trust and confidence is one of our top priorities. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

When you apply for membership or avail of our loan products and facilities, SCSLAI may collect the following personal information to better serve you:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Landline/Mobile Phone Numbers and Other Contact Details
  • CCTV Footage for Security Purposes
  • Payment Information
  • Salary/Wage from Your Source(s) of Livelihood

In addition to the information listed above, SCSLAI may also collect data related to your transactions, account activity, interactions with the Association, and other personal information required by SCSLAI or government regulators such as the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP).

2. Use of Information

Purpose of Processing

Your personal information is used to process transactions, manage your accounts, provide personalized financial services, prevent fraud, perform risk management, and comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

SCSLAI collects and stores your personal and sensitive information for identification, credit checking, verification, and determining your qualification to avail of the Association's products and services.

3. Data Sharing

There may be instances when your personal information will be shared with appropriate government agencies, regulatory bodies, and trusted third-party service providers to comply with reporting requirements or support the development and improvement of SCSLAI's information systems.

By availing of our products and services, you provide your express and continuing consent to allow these authorized entities to process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Product Offering
  • Responding to Inquiries
  • Participation in Promotions
  • Conducting Surveys
  • Collection Initiatives of SCSLAI

SCSLAI does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement strict physical, electronic, and procedural security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our systems are regularly monitored and updated to maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and security of your data.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information held by SCSLAI. You may also opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights or if you have questions regarding your privacy, you may contact our Data Protection Officers:

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SCSLAI may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your online experience, analyze website usage, and enhance our online services, including our Facebook page, Messenger, and other social media platforms. You may manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Updates to the Data Privacy Statement

This Data Privacy Statement may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our privacy practices or regulatory requirements. Any material updates will be communicated through our official Facebook page, website, or other authorized communication channels.

By continuing to use our services, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Data Privacy Statement.

The security of your personal information remains our priority. We protect your data by maintaining appropriate physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with applicable laws. We also ensure that our employees receive proper training in the responsible handling of personal information.
